Department of UN-SKILLED Event

FEST

Maximum 2 participants per team. Only 2 teams per college is allowed Students can exhibit their puzzle solving skills based on physics concepts.

MIME

Maximum 4 participants per team. Students can explain any physics concept through mime

DEBATE

Each team consists of 3 participants. There will be a screening round. Top 2 teams will be selected for the final round. Topics will be given on the spot and 10 minutes will be given for preparation.

COLLAGE

Single participant. Students have to bring the required materials. Participants have to prepare the collage on the spot in the given time.

RAPID FIRE ROUND

Each team consists of 3 participants. Event will be based on Physics theme.

PHYSICS MEMES

Individual participant. Students have to prepare the memes on the spot and showcase their talent.

PHYSICS CHARADES

Maximum 3 participants per team. There will be a screening round. 'Words' related to physics will be given on the spot to act and find.
